Breed details

The Tibetan Mastiff is a large and impressive dog breed that originated in Tibet and has been used for centuries as a guard dog for families and livestock. They are known for their imposing appearance, loyal nature, and protective instincts.

Tibetan Mastiffs are one of the largest dog breeds, with males typically weighing between 100-160 pounds and standing 26-30 inches tall at the shoulder. They have a thick, double coat that can be black, brown, or blue-gray in color, and a mane of fur around their neck that gives them a majestic appearance.
